Penguin Love

This is a commission for a series of paintings from different artists to be framed on a wall, where the only criteria was it had to involve a penguin. So I decided I'd do a painting about how Papa penguin nurtures love to baby penguin.

 

Miscellaneous Process Images

For the character I made some explorations with ink and brushes, and once I found the essence, moved to digital paper. Once I found the type of characters I was going to go with, roughed out the colour and shapes, and went for it.
